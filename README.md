## 📌 Project Overview
**Bloomy** is a modern perfume brand offering long-lasting fragrances inspired by luxury scents at affordable prices.  
This project focuses on building a strong brand identity, defining market positioning, creating a content strategy, and executing a digital marketing plan to drive awareness and sales.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Build a clear and strong brand identity.
- Position Bloomy as a premium yet affordable alternative.
- Increase brand awareness through social media.
- Generate sales using paid advertising campaigns.

---

## 📦 Project Scope
- Brand identity development.
- Product positioning strategy.
- Content strategy and planning.
- Paid advertising plan.
- Digital launch strategy.

**Final Deliverable:**  
A complete digital launch plan including branding, content strategy, and paid advertising strategy.
