## 📌 Project Overview
**Aura Accessories** is a modern jewelry brand based in Egypt, specializing in personalized and customizable accessories that reflect individual identity and emotions.  

Unlike traditional accessory brands, Aura focuses on transforming simple jewelry into meaningful pieces that tell a story — whether it’s a name, a memory, or a special connection.

The brand aims to bridge the gap between **affordable fashion** and **emotional personalization**, offering high-quality designs at competitive prices.
